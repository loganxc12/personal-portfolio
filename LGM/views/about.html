Creative Vision + Attention to detail

I'm a 23 year old develper from Maine with big ambitions and varied

A paradox of sorts, I’m a web developer who loves being in nature,
practices handstands and backflips in his spare time, and cares
deeply about the health of the planet. I believe this gives me a
unique perspective from which to interface with the digital world.

A "creative-type" at heart, I approah my work from a right-brained, idea
oriented place – thinking about the why, what and how behind the code I
write. I also have a firm grasp of the technical fundamentals which, coupled
with my borderline obsessive attention to detail, allows me to create clean
and impactful digital experiences. I still have a ton to learn, but I'm proud
of the apps I've built thus far.
